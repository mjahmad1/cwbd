QURANER FARIWALA

Quran Distribution Project for Students in Flood-Affected Areas

Total Budget: 22,600,000 BDT

1. Project Objectives:

  • Distribute the Quran among students in flood-affected areas.
  • Promote religious education among students.
  • Enhance religious values and moral education.
  • Encourage interest in education in flood-affected areas.

2. Target Group:

  • Students in flood-affected areas (ages 6-18 years).
  • Local mosques and religious institutions.
  • Members of the local community.

3. Project Duration:

  • Total duration: 6 months.
  • Timeline for project phases:
    • Printing: 1 month
    • Transportation: 2 months
    • Distribution: 3 months

4. Project Phases:

1. Printing:

  • Contract with a reliable publishing house for printing the Quran.
  • Test samples to ensure printing quality.
  • Determine the quantity to be printed (60,000 copies).

2. Transportation:

  • Contract with a transportation company to deliver the printed Qurans to flood-affected areas.
  • Set transportation schedules and ensure timely delivery.
  • Ensure the safety and security of the flood-affected areas.

3. Distribution:

  • Plan the distribution of the Quran in local schools and mosques.
  • Communicate with students and their guardians.
  • Discuss the importance of religious education during distribution.

4. Other Costs:

  • Administrative costs, staff expenses, etc.: 200,000 BDT.

Total Budget:

  • Printing costs: 21,600,000 BDT
  • Transportation costs: 500,000 BDT
  • Distribution costs: 300,000 BDT
  • Other costs: 200,000 BDT
  • Total Budget: 22,600,000 BDT

6. Project Success Evaluation:

  • Collect feedback from students after distribution.
  • Gather opinions from the local community about the spread of religious education.
  • Analyze project outcomes and make recommendations for future projects.
