Project Plan: Nutritional Food Supply for Flood-Affected Hifz Students

Total Budget - 2,20,00,000 (BDT)

Project Name:

Nutritional Food Supply for Flood-Affected Hifz Students

Objective:

To provide nutritional food to 60,000 Hifz students in flood-affected areas.

Duration:

3 months

Activities:

Initial Assessment:

  • Identification of affected areas
  • Preparation of student lists

Food Collection:

  • Collection and storage of nutritional food items
  • Ensuring the quality of food items

Distribution Plan:

  • Logistics planning for distribution
  • Arrangements for transportation and storage

Distribution:

  • Distribution of food items to students
  • Scheduling and location determination for distribution

Feedback and Evaluation:

  • Evaluation of program outcomes
  • Collection of feedback from students and their families

Budget Plan:

Category Amount (BDT)
Initial Assessment 5,00,000
Food Collection and Purchase 1,50,00,000
Storage and Transportation 30,00,000
Distribution Costs 20,00,000
Administrative Costs 10,00,000
Feedback and Evaluation 5,00,000
Total 2,20,00,000

Detailed Description:

Initial Assessment:

  • Field surveys and data collection
  • Preparation of student lists

Food Collection and Purchase:

  • Collection of nutritional food items such as rice, lentils, oil, dried fruits, etc.
  • Ensuring the quality of food items

Storage and Transportation:

  • Renting warehouses for food storage
  • Renting vehicles for transportation

Distribution:

  • Distribution of food items to students
  • Scheduling and location determination for distribution

Administrative Costs:

  • Salaries of staff and other administrative expenses

Feedback and Evaluation:

  • Evaluation of program outcomes
  • Collection of feedback from students and their families

FAQ (Questions and Answers):

Question 1: Who are the beneficiaries of this project?

Answer: The beneficiaries of the project are 60,000 flood-affected Hifz students.

Question 2: What is the schedule for food distribution?

Answer: Food distribution will be completed within 3 months according to the scheduled timeline.

Question 3: How will the food items be collected?

Answer: Nutritional food items will be collected from local farmers and markets.

Question 4: What is the budget for the project?

Answer: The total budget for the project is 2,20,00,000 BDT.

Question 5: How will feedback be collected?

Answer: Feedback will be collected through direct interviews with students and their families.

Question 6: What are the main objectives of providing food in this project?

Answer: The objectives of providing food are:

  • Meeting nutritional needs
  • Maintaining continuity of education
  • Reducing the impact of floods
  • Fulfilling social responsibility
  • Enhancing social cohesion
