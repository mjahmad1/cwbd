Temporary Education Program Project for Flood-Affected Hifz Educational Institutions

Total Budget - 460,000,000 (BDT)

Objective:

To conduct temporary education programs for students of 900 Hifz educational institutions in flood-affected areas.

Duration:

6 months

Activities:

  1. Initial Assessment:
    • Identification of affected educational institutions
    • Preparation of student lists
  2. Establishment of Temporary Education Centers:
    • Setting up temporary tents or sheds
    • Procurement of necessary furniture and educational materials
  3. Teacher Recruitment and Training:
    • Recruitment of temporary teachers
    • Providing training to teachers
  4. Conducting Educational Activities:
    • Regular classes
    • Providing psychological support to students
  5. Feedback and Evaluation:
    • Evaluation of program outcomes
    • Collecting feedback from students and their families
